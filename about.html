Our Methods

Despite building this product in less than six days, we did our best to implement the Agile methodology as well as a few other software development techniques. We began by creating a user persona, and story-mapping the users’ product experience from beginning to end. We then wire-framed our concept, and came up with a 3-4 page website. Throughout this process, we implemented 1-day sprints. After our daily sprints, we got together for a mini-retrospective to discuss our roadblocks, and any other issues we had for that day. Our day-to-day workflow consisted of collaborative mob-programming, as well as individual programming. We also ensured that each team-member was contributing equally to the project, and getting the experience that she needed to improve her skills. Overall, we thoroughly enjoyed the process of creating this product, and believe that the applied best practices and methodologies allowed for a great experience.
